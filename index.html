Welcome to Green Minds

Empowering environmental sustainability in Kwale County, Kenya

Green Minds Youth Initiative is a youth-led organization based in Waa Location, Kwale County, Kenya, dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability, climate action, and community empowerment.

Through innovative projects, mentorship, and partnerships, we aim to create resilient communities that thrive in harmony with nature.

Learn More

Our Mission

Creating lasting environmental impact through youth empowerment and in school children engagement.

We nurture youth-driven solutions for climate resilience, waste management, and sustainable livelihoods through education, innovation, and community engagement.

Our approach combines traditional knowledge with modern environmental practices to create sustainable solutions.

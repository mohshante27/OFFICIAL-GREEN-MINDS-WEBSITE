Success Story: Juma's Farm Transformation

Juma Mohamed, a farmer from Mwabungu, has transformed his 2-acre farm from struggling conventional agriculture to a thriving sustainable enterprise.

"Before joining this program, I was spending most of my income on chemical fertilizers and pesticides. My soil was getting poorer each season. Now, I use compost from my farm waste, practice crop rotation, and have planted fruit trees that provide shade and additional income. My yields have increased by 50%, and my production costs have reduced significantly," Juma shares.

Juma now mentors other farmers in his community and has established a demonstration plot that attracts visitors from neighboring villages.

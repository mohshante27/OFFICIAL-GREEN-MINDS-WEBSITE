Success Story: Diani Beach Revival

Diani Beach, one of Kenya's most famous beaches, was facing serious pollution challenges from plastic waste and improper waste management. Through our partnership with Diani Youth Beach Cleaning & Management Service, we've transformed the beach into a cleaner, healthier environment.

"When we started, we were collecting over 200kg of plastic waste every week from just one kilometer of beach. Now, through regular cleanups and community education, we've reduced that to about 50kg per week. Tourists are noticing the difference and local businesses are more engaged in keeping the beach clean," says beach manager Ali Hassan.

The program has created 15 permanent jobs for youth in beach management and waste collection, turning an environmental challenge into an economic opportunity.

Our Story

Green Minds Youth Initiative was born from a shared passion for environmental conservation and youth empowerment in Kwale County, Kenya. Founded by Alice Loiton, our organization brings together young people who are committed to creating sustainable solutions for environmental challenges.

Starting as a small community group in Waa Location, we have grown into a recognized youth-led organization making tangible impacts in schools and communities across Kwale County.

Our Mission

To nurture youth-driven solutions for climate resilience, waste management, and sustainable livelihoods through education, innovation, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A green, empowered, and sustainable future where communities thrive in harmony with nature, and youth are at the forefront of environmental stewardship.

Our Core Values

The Green Minds Youth Initiative is committed to a set of core values that guide all activities, interactions, and objectives. These values define the organizational culture and serve as the foundation for achieving our mission.

Environmental Stewardship

We are dedicated to protecting, conserving, and restoring the environment for current and future generations. Our activities are focused on promoting environmental awareness, sustainable practices, and responsible resource management.

Community Empowerment

We believe in empowering local communities, especially young people, to actively participate in climate action. Through education, skills development, and inclusive engagement, we aim to build resilient, environmentally-conscious communities.

Integrity and Transparency

Integrity and honesty are at the core of our operations. We commit to transparency in our decision-making processes, financial management, and communications, ensuring accountability to our members and supporters.

Inclusivity and Respect

We celebrate diversity and foster an environment where everyone feels respected, valued, and heard. We are inclusive in our work, regardless of background, and seek to represent the diverse voices of Kwale County and neighboring regions.

Collaboration and Partnership

We value partnerships and actively seek collaboration with organizations, government bodies, and individuals who share our vision. Working together strengthens our impact and enhances our ability to achieve sustainable climate solutions.

Innovation and Learning

We are committed to continual learning and innovation in our approach to climate justice. By staying informed and embracing new ideas, we can adapt to the evolving needs of the community and the environment.

Success Story: Plastic Bottle Revolution

In Kombani, plastic bottles were a major environmental problem clogging drainage systems and polluting landscapes. Through our upcycling program, we trained youth groups to transform these bottles into eco-bricks for construction and creative art pieces.

"We used to see plastic bottles as trash, but now we see them as building materials. Our youth group has built a community shade using eco-bricks and we're earning income from selling decorative items made from upcycled plastics," says Amina, a youth leader.

The project has diverted over 2 tonnes of plastic from the environment and created sustainable income for 15 young people in the community.
